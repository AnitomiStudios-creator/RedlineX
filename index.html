Formula One Experience

Redline X is a Formula One-inspired racing simulation focused on high-speed precision, aerodynamics, and elite motorsport engineering. Drive cutting-edge open-wheel cars across technical circuits designed to challenge skill, consistency, and race strategy. Challenge for the win and the monopoly over the pinacle of motorsport

First Track: Albert Park

The first playable track in Early Access will be the Albert Park Circuit in Melbourne — a fast and flowing street circuit that opens the Formula One season. The circuit will be highly high res but still lightweight with trying to get a much real feel as possible

Early Access Launch

Redline X will launch with core gameplay systems, one circuit, and continuous updates bringing new cars, tracks, and features. This will ensure the games possibilites and fix minor bugs before the realease of whole game
