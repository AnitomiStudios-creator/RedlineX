Inspired by Formula One

Redline X is built around the spirit of Formula One — the pinnacle of motorsport. The game focuses entirely on open-wheel Formula racing with high-downforce cars, precision cornering, and technical circuits inspired by the real F1 calendar.

First Early Access Circuit

The first playable circuit in Redline X Early Access will be the iconic Albert Park Circuit in Melbourne, Australia — home of the Australian Grand Prix.

Players will experience the fast chicanes, technical braking zones, and flowing layout that makes Albert Park one of Formula One’s most exciting opening races of the season.

Early Access Launch

Redline X will launch in Early Access as development progresses. Players will gain access to the first cars, physics systems, and the Albert Park circuit while the game continues to expand with new tracks, improved mechanics, and more realistic racing systems.
