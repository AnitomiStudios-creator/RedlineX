About the Game

Redline X is a high-speed racing experience focused on precision driving and competitive motorsport. Race across challenging circuits, master high-performance machines, and compete for pole position. Built with a passion for racing simulations, the game focuses on immersive environments, fast gameplay, and intense racing action.

Redline X is developed by Anitomi Studios, an indie game development studio focused on motorsport games and simulation environments. The studio aims to create immersive racing experiences inspired by real-world motorsport and modern racing simulators.
