Inspired by Formula One

Redline X is built around the spirit of Formula One — the pinnacle of motorsport. Every element of the game focuses on the engineering, speed, and precision that defines modern F1 racing. From high-downforce open wheel cars to world famous circuits, Redline X aims to capture the intensity of Formula One competition. Players will experience the challenge of mastering technical corners, maximizing speed on long straights, and managing racing lines just like professional F1 drivers.

First Early Access Circuit

The first playable circuit in Redline X Early Access will be the legendary Albert Park Circuit in Melbourne, Australia — home of the Australian Grand Prix and one of the most exciting street circuits in Formula One.

Players will be able to race on this circuit as the first testing ground for the Redline X physics engine and racing mechanics. Redline X is based on the Godot Engine aiming to give good performance and graphics even to low end deivces

Early Access Launch

Redline X will launch in Early Access as development progresses. Players will gain access to the first cars, physics systems, and the Albert Park circuit while the game continues to expand with new tracks, improved mechanics, and more realistic racing systems.
