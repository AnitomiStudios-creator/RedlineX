Inspired by Formula One

Redline X is built around the core principles of Formula One — precision, aerodynamics, and extreme speed. Experience open-wheel racing like never before with realistic physics and high-speed circuits.

First Early Access Circuit

The first track in Redline X Early Access will be the Albert Park Circuit, Melbourne — home of the Australian Grand Prix.

Early Access Launch

Redline X will launch in Early Access with core gameplay systems, initial vehicles, and the Albert Park circuit. More content will be added over time.
